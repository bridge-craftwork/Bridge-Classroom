Understand your club game results, board by board

After your club game, find out not just how you scored — but why. Every board is categorized by cause: good play, lucky result, bidding error, declarer error, or defense. Spot your patterns over time.

Board-by-board breakdown

Score, matchpoint percentage, field contract, and declarer tricks vs. field average — for every board you played.

Cause analysis

Each board is tagged: Good skill, Lucky, Auction problem, Play error, Defense error, or Unlucky. Know which areas to work on.

Links to BBO Hand Viewer

Click any board number to open the full hand record in BBO's hand viewer for deeper review.

How to use it today
1

After your club game, download your .PBN and .BWS files from ACBL Live.

2

Download the CLI for your computer below and run it with your two files as input.

3

Get a formatted board-by-board analysis with cause categorization and BBO links.

A web version is in development — upload your files in the browser and get the same analysis without any downloads. No release date yet, but the core analysis is complete.
