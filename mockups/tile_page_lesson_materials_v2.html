Supporting materials for each Baker Bridge lesson — matched to the online practice hands your students are using in the app. Organized by lesson, then by set size and table format.

About the teaching format variants

Each lesson has a "hot seat" — the player whose decision is being practiced (usually declarer or opener). Materials come in three variants so the hot seat rotates correctly: Full Table (face-to-face, rotates N/E/S/W), South (online, one player per table), and North-South (online, two players per table). The folder structure inside each lesson shows you exactly which files to use.
